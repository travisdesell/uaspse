NSF BDSPOKES: Unmanned Aerial Systems, Plant Sciences and Education

The UASPSE NSF Big Data Hubs Spoke Project aims to contribute in solving sustainable global food security by coordinating efforts by the academic, industrial, and governmental sectors to automate Big Data lifecycles, improve access to data assets, and train individuals with relevant skills and expertise.
