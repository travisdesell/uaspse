Data Collection

Using your LinkedIn credentials to create a UPSiE Member Account will give DigitalAg.org access to all the information in your LinkedIn profile. By signing in, you authorize DigitalAg.org to collect this information and store it in a database.

Data Removal

To remove your LinkedIn profile information from the DigitalAg.org database and disenroll as a UPSiE Member, click the 'Remove UPSiE Account' located on your UPSiE Member Profile. Doing so will perminently remove your LinkedIn data from the database. You may re-join the UPSiE Membership at any time.

For what purpose will my data be used?

DigitalAg.org will use this data to identify and suggest potential partnerships and disseminate project information to UPSiE Members. Your information will not be shared with any third parties and we will not send you any unsolicited email. After registering, you can opt-in to occasional emails about UPSiE events and activities from your account page.
